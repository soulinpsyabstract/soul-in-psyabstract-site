1 · Who We Are

Soul In PsyAbstract is an independent art and handmade-products store operated by Aelin AquaSoul, based in Eilat, Israel. Website: soulinpsyabstract.store. Contact: sipa-core@sipa-os.org

2 · Data We Collect

When you interact with our store or contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact data — name, email address, phone number (only when you provide it voluntarily via WhatsApp, email, or contact forms).
  • Order data — shipping address, items ordered, payment confirmation references.
  • Usage data — pages visited, device type, browser (collected via PostHog analytics — see §4).
  • Communications — messages you send us via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email.

We do not collect payment card details directly — payments are processed by PayPal or handled via external services.

3 · How We Use Your Data

  • To process and fulfill orders.
  • To communicate with you about your order status.
  • To improve our website and understand visitor behaviour (analytics).
  • To respond to enquiries and support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

4 · Third-Party Services

We use the following services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • PostHog — website analytics (posthog.com/privacy)
  • PayPal — payment processing (paypal.com/privacy)
  • Intercom — customer messaging (intercom.com/legal/privacy)
  • WhatsApp / Meta — order communication (whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy)
  • Instagram / Meta — social communications (privacycenter.instagram.com)
  • Gumroad — digital product delivery (gumroad.com/privacy)

5 · Cookies

Our website uses cookies and local storage for analytics (PostHog) and to remember your preferences (language setting). You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

6 · Your Rights

Under GDPR and Israeli Privacy Protection Law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a portable format.
  • Objection — object to certain processing (e.g. marketing).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sipa-core@sipa-os.org.
